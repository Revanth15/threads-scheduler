## Tips & Best Practices

### How to Write Threads That Get Engagement
The best Threads posts are short, punchy, and end with a question. People scroll fast — your first line needs to stop them.

Tips:
- Lead with the most interesting part
- Use line breaks (Threads loves white space)
- End with a question or call to action
- Don't over-hashtag — 2-3 max

Tags: #tips #contentmarketing #threads
Priority: high

### The Best Times to Post on Threads
Data shows morning posts (7-9am) perform best for motivation and productivity content. Evening posts (5-7pm) work better for reflective, thought-provoking content.

Tags: #timing #strategy #socialmedia
Priority: medium

### Why Consistency Beats Virality
Going viral once doesn't build a following. Posting consistently every day does. Show up every day with something useful and the algorithm will reward you.

Tags: #growth #consistency #strategy
Priority: medium
